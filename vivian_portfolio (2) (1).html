About Me

Bridging Technology, Finance & Education

I am Vivian Atieno Odhiambo, a dedicated lecturer at KENIA COLLEGE, where I teach Information & Communication Technology (ICT) and Certified Public Accountancy (CPA). My mission is to equip students with both the technical acumen and financial literacy to thrive in today's digital economy.

With hands-on expertise in digital freelancing platforms including Ajira Digital and BrighterMonday, I bring real-world relevance to every lesson — connecting classroom theory to the opportunities of the modern gig and corporate economy.

I am certified, committed, and passionate about creating transformative learning experiences that open doors for my students — and I am eager to bring this energy to new institutional opportunities.
